About me

I was born in Colombia on December 1988. I had the fortune to grow up in the tranquility of the countryside in a large and lovely family. I studied Industrial Engineering in Colombia and I got my degree in 2013. I am a self-taught developer learning new things every day. Currently, I am happily married and I am living in Sweden with my husband.

In my spare time, I like drawing using CSS, also I use to go to the gym, enjoy dancing and doing exercises that require concentration. I love great food and explore new flavors. I appreciate sharing moments with my friends and family. Traveling, hiking and discovering new places are my favorite hobbies.
